Controls

Any controller

  • In The first picure above you can see a controller with different functions. Each button you press will apply to the game.
    What you see on the picture are all the controls of the game. The first would you need to learn is
    all the controls since you have to be fast and you have to be able to play without having to look down like shifting a manual car.
    It is always a good idea to use your own settings and not try to copy someone and use the settings that are comfortable for you. Anything
    that allows you to hold boost and jump at the same time is good for a start.


    • Camera Settings

    Camera

  • If you want to improve fast i would recommend to look for some good camera settings. In the pictures above i have put in 2 different settings
    from pro players that you could use if you would like but before you do lets go over what everything does
    A greater camera distance provides a broader view of the field. A camera higher gives you a better
    viw of the eild and the ball when its in the air and ground

  • The angle affects how much of the field you can see and can influence your perception of the ball's position.
    Field of View a wider one allows you to see more of the play area which is crucial for awareness and positioning
    Higher stiffness keeps the camera closer to the car, making it easier to control during fast
    maneuvers while lower allows for smoother camera movement
    Swivel speed, this setting affect how quickly the camre moves when you turn your car.
    Transition speed, this setting controls the speed at which the camera switches from the ball cam to car cam.
    Ball cam, use ball cam "toggle with the "Y" button on xbox or the "triangel" on playstation"
    to keep the camera focused on the ball or car.
